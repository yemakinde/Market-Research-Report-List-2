<p><p>SFP modules are small form-factor pluggable transceivers used in networking equipment. Singlemode SFP modules are designed for long-distance transmission over single-mode fiber optic cables, while multimode SFP modules are used for shorter distances over multimode fiber optic cables. Singlemode SFP modules are more expensive but offer higher bandwidth and longer reach, making them suitable for enterprise environments. Multimode SFP modules are more cost-effective and are commonly used in data centers and short-distance communication applications.</p></p>
