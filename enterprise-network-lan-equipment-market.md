<p><ul><li>Token Ring</li><li>Fiber Distributed Data Interface (FDDI)</li><li>ARCNET</li><li>Ethernet</li><li>Wireless LAN (WLAN) or Wi-Fi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise Network LAN Equipment market encompasses various types of technologies including Token Ring, Fiber Distributed Data Interface (FDDI), ARCNET, Ethernet, Wireless LAN (WLAN) or Wi-Fi. Token Ring utilizes a token-passing protocol for communication, while FDDI offers high-speed data transmission over optical fiber. ARCNET is known for its deterministic behavior and simplicity. Ethernet is the most commonly used LAN technology. Wireless LAN or Wi-Fi technology enables wireless connectivity within a network, allowing for flexibility and mobility. Each of these technologies serves specific needs within an enterprise network.</p></p>
