<p><p>Programmable Silicon market includes Field-Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) and Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs). FPGAs are highly versatile and can be reprogrammed for various applications, making them ideal for prototyping and low- to mid-volume production. CPLDs are typically used for smaller, less complex designs and are more cost-effective for simpler tasks. Both markets cater to the demand for customizable silicon solutions that offer flexibility and rapid development cycles, with FPGAs targeting higher-end applications and CPLDs targeting simpler, more cost-sensitive projects.</p></p>
