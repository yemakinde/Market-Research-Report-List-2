<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Parallel Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical synthesizers can be categorized into two main types: single mode and parallel mode. In single mode synthesizers, one compound or reaction is produced at a time, making them ideal for small-scale production or research purposes. On the other hand, parallel mode synthesizers are capable of producing multiple compounds or reactions simultaneously, increasing efficiency and throughput. This makes them suitable for high-throughput screening, combinatorial chemistry, and other applications that require the synthesis of multiple compounds in parallel.</p></p>
