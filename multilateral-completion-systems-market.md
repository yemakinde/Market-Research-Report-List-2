<p><p>Multilateral completion systems are classified into six levels based on their complexity and functionality. TAML Level 1 systems are basic with limited access to lateral boreholes. TAML Level 2 systems have enhanced capabilities for branching off lateral bores. TAML Level 3 systems offer more advanced branching options. TAML Level 4 systems are highly versatile with improved hydraulic fracturing abilities. TAML Level 5 systems are customizable for specific wellbore needs. TAML Level 6 systems are cutting-edge with the highest degree of complexity and functionality.</p></p>
