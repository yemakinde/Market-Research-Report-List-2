<p><p>The plastic screws and bolts market can be classified into two main types - plastic screws and plastic bolts. Plastic screws are fasteners with helical ridges that are designed to be screwed into a pre-drilled hole to securely hold objects together. Plastic bolts, on the other hand, are fasteners with external threading and a square or hexagonal head that are used with a nut to create a strong joint. Both plastic screws and plastic bolts offer lightweight, non-corrosive, and cost-effective alternatives to traditional metal fasteners.</p></p>
