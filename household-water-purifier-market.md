<p><p>Household water purifiers can be categorized into three main types: Reverse Osmosis (RO) water purifiers, Ultraviolet (UV) water purifiers, and others. RO water purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants from water, while UV water purifiers use ultraviolet light to kill harmful bacteria and viruses. Other types of household water purifiers may include activated carbon filters, ion exchange systems, or distillation units. Each type of water purifier offers unique advantages in terms of purifying water and ensuring clean drinking water for households.</p></p>
