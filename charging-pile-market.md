<p><p>Charging piles are categorized into two main types based on the type of current they use: AC charging pile and DC charging pile. AC charging piles provide electricity to electric vehicles using alternating current, while DC charging piles use direct current. AC charging piles are generally slower but are more common and cost-effective, making them suitable for residential and commercial use. Conversely, DC charging piles are faster and more efficient, making them ideal for public charging stations and long-distance travel.</p></p>
