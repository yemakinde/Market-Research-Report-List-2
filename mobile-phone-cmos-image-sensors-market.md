<p><strong>The Mobile Phone CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stacked CMOS</li><li>Back-illuminated CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Phone CMOS Image Sensors market can be classified into two major types: Stacked CMOS and Back-illuminated CMOS. Stacked CMOS image sensors have a vertical integration of circuitry, allowing for higher pixel density and improved image quality. On the other hand, Back-illuminated CMOS sensors have a design where the light-sensitive area is positioned at the back of the sensor, enabling better light sensitivity and low-light performance. Both types of sensors are crucial for enhancing the quality and performance of mobile phone cameras.</p></p>
