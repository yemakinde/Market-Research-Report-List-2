<p><p>Magnetic-field sensing ICs are used for measuring magnetic fields and are categorized into four main types: Hall Effect Sensing ICs, TMR Sensing ICs, AMR Sensing ICs, and GMR Sensing ICs. Hall Effect ICs utilize the Hall effect to measure magnetic fields. TMR ICs use tunneling magnetoresistance to detect changes in magnetic fields. AMR ICs rely on anisotropic magnetoresistance for sensing magnetic fields. GMR ICs use giant magnetoresistance for high sensitivity in detecting magnetic fields. Each type has unique characteristics suited for different applications.</p></p>
