<p><p>An Artificial Cardiopulmonary Machine is a medical device that provides temporary support to a patient's heart and lungs during cardiac or respiratory failure. It is often used in critical care settings such as intensive care units (ICUs) or operating rooms. The machine works by oxygenating the blood and pumping it through the body, essentially performing the functions of the heart and lungs.</p><p>The Artificial Cardiopulmonary Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period. The Single Roller Pump HLM is a simpler design, with a single roller pushing the blood through the machine. On the other hand, the Double Roller Pump HLM has two rollers working in tandem to provide a more consistent flow of blood during cardiac procedures. Both types have their advantages and are used in different medical settings based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
