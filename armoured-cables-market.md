<p><p>Armoured cables are cables with added protection through layers of armouring, such as Aluminium Wire Armour and Steel Wire Armour. Aluminium Wire Armour is made with aluminium wires twisted around the cable to provide protection against mechanical damage and corrosion. Steel Wire Armour, on the other hand, uses steel wires for increased strength and durability. Both types of armour provide protection to the cables, making them suitable for harsh environments and ensuring reliable transmission of electricity or data.</p></p>
