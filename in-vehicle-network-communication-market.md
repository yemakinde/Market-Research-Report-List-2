<p><p>In-vehicle network communication market includes two main types: in-car wired connection and out-car wireless connection. In-car wired connection refers to the communication systems, such as Controller Area Network (CAN) bus, that are physically connected within the vehicle to enable data transfer between various electronic components. On the other hand, out-car wireless connection involves wireless networks, like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, that allow communication between the vehicle and external devices or networks outside of the vehicle, enhancing connectivity and providing more advanced functionalities.</p></p>
