<p><p>PoE Midspans, also known as Power over Ethernet Midspans, are devices used to deliver power to Ethernet devices over a network cable. These devices are typically used in situations where Power over Ethernet (PoE) is not natively supported by network switches or other network infrastructure. PoE Midspans inject power into the Ethernet cable, allowing devices such as IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones to receive power and data through a single cable.</p><p>The PoE Midspans Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing adoption of PoE technology in various industries such as healthcare, retail, and manufacturing. The demand for PoE Midspans is also being driven by the rising deployment of IP cameras and other Ethernet-powered devices for security and surveillance purposes.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the PoE Midspans market is the growing popularity of high-power PoE solutions that can deliver more than 60 watts of power to devices. This trend is driven by the increasing power requirements of advanced devices such as pan-tilt-zoom cameras and outdoor access points. Manufacturers in the PoE Midspans market are focusing on developing innovative solutions that can meet the power needs of these devices while maintaining efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
