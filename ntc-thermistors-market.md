<p><p>NTC thermistors are available in various types to cater to different applications. Radial type thermistors are commonly used in temperature sensing applications. Diode type thermistors have a unique electrical property that allows them to operate as both a temperature sensor and a diode. Wire bonding type thermistors are ideal for high-reliability applications. Film type thermistors are thin and flexible, making them suitable for temperature measurement in tight spaces. SMD type thermistors are surface mountable for easy installation. Wire type thermistors are robust and durable. Chip in glass type thermistors offer precise temperature measurement in a compact package.</p></p>
