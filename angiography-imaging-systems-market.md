<p><p>Angiography Imaging Systems refer to medical devices used to visualize blood vessels in the body. There are four main types of angiography imaging systems: X-Ray Angiography, CT Angiography, MRI Angiography, and MR-CT Angiography. X-Ray Angiography uses X-rays to create detailed images of blood vessels. CT Angiography involves the use of a CT scanner to produce cross-sectional images of blood vessels. MRI Angiography uses magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images. MR-CT Angiography combines the benefits of both MRI and CT technologies for enhanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>
