<p><strong>The Consumer 3D Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>iToF</li><li>dToF</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer 3D vision sensor market is segmented into types such as Structured Light, iTOF (indirect Time of Flight), dToF (direct Time of Flight), and Others. Structured Light technology uses patterns of light to measure depth and create 3D images. iTOF sensors use laser pulses to calculate distance, while dToF sensors directly measure the time taken for light to bounce back. Other types may include stereo vision or photometric stereo methods. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the consumer 3D vision sensor market.</p></p>
