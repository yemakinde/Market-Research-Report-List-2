<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) market includes two main types: Layer Stranding Structure OPGW and Central Tube Structure OPGW. Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of optical fibers wrapped around the central strength member in layers, providing a flexible design for high-voltage power lines. Central Tube Structure OPGW features optical fibers housed inside a central aluminum tube, offering enhanced protection and stability. Both types are widely used in the power transmission industry for improving communication capabilities and monitoring systems on overhead lines.</p></p>
