<p><p>Heavy duty power connectors are typically classified into four main types: box mount, panel mount, surface mount, and other. Box mount connectors are designed to be mounted in an enclosure or box, panel mount connectors are mounted directly on a panel or surface, and surface mount connectors are mounted directly onto a PCB or other surface. The category of "other" encompasses connectors that may have unique mounting requirements or configurations that do not fit into the previous three categories. Different applications and industries may require different types of heavy duty power connectors depending on their specific needs and constraints.</p></p>
