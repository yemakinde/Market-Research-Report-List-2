<p><p>Edge computing is a decentralized computing infrastructure where data processing is done closer to the data source, rather than relying on a centralized data center. This enables faster data processing, reduced latency, and improved efficiency, making it ideal for applications that require real-time data analysis and processing.</p><p>The Edge Computing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 17.80% during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) devices and the rising demand for low-latency data processing are driving the growth of the edge computing market. Additionally, the proliferation of 5G networks is further fueling the demand for edge computing solutions to support the massive amounts of data generated by connected devices.</p><p>Some of the key trends shaping the edge computing market include the convergence of edge computing with artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, the development of edge computing platforms for specific industries such as healthcare and manufacturing, and the emergence of edge-as-a-service offerings. Overall, the edge computing market is poised for rapid growth as organizations seek to leverage the benefits of decentralized data processing for improved efficiency and performance.</p></p>
