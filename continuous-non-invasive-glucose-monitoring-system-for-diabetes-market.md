<p><p>Continuous Non-invasive Glucose Monitoring System (CNGMS) for diabetes is a technology that allows individuals to monitor their glucose levels without the need for frequent finger pricks. There are two main types of CNGMS available in the market: Wearable CNGMS and Implantable CNGMS. </p><p>Wearable CNGMS are devices that can be worn on the body and provide continuous monitoring of glucose levels through sensors. These devices are convenient and easy to use, making them popular among diabetes patients.</p><p>Implantable CNGMS, on the other hand, are devices that are surgically implanted under the skin to monitor glucose levels continuously. While these devices may offer more accurate readings, they are more invasive and require a surgical procedure for placement. </p><p>Both types of CNGMS are gaining popularity in the market as they offer a more convenient and less painful way for diabetes patients to monitor their glucose levels regularly.</p></p>
