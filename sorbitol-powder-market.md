<p><strong>The Sorbitol Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pan-agglomerated Sorbitol</li><li>Spray-dried Sorbitol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sorbitol powder is available in the market in two main types: pan-agglomerated sorbitol and spray-dried sorbitol. Pan-agglomerated sorbitol is produced through a process where sorbitol particles are agglomerated using a pan process, resulting in a free-flowing powder. On the other hand, spray-dried sorbitol is created by spraying a sorbitol solution into a hot airstream, which dries the liquid droplets into a fine powder. Both types of sorbitol powder have various applications in the food and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
