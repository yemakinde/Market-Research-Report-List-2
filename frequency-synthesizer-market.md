<p><ul><li>Analog Frequency Synthesizer</li><li>Digital Frequency Synthesizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency synthesizers are devices used to generate an output signal with a frequency that is determined by an input reference signal. Analog frequency synthesizers use analog components to generate the desired frequency, while digital frequency synthesizers use digital processing techniques. Analog synthesizers offer continuous tuning capabilities and are often used in applications that require high spectral purity. On the other hand, digital synthesizers offer precise frequency control and can easily integrate with digital systems for complex signal processing tasks.</p></p>
