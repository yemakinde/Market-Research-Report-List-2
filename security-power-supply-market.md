<p><p>Security power supply market can be broadly classified into two types - DC-DC and AC-DC markets. DC-DC power supplies convert one DC voltage to another, providing a stable output voltage for security applications. This type of power supply is widely used in devices that require different voltages for efficient operation. On the other hand, AC-DC power supplies convert AC voltage to DC voltage, suitable for powering security systems that run on AC power sources. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable power supply for security devices.</p></p>
