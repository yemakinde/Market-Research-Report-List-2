<p><p>Externally Pressurized Expansion Joints are used in piping systems to absorb thermal expansion and vibration, preventing damage to the system. There are two main types of Externally Pressurized Expansion Joints - Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type joints are connected using bolted flanges, allowing for easy installation and removal. Welding Type joints are welded directly into the piping system, providing a more permanent solution. Both types offer flexibility and durability in managing the movement of the piping system under pressure.</p></p>
