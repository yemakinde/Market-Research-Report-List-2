<p><strong>The Rubber Injection Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber injection machines are classified into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical type machines have a vertical mold clamping system, making them ideal for precision molding and overmolding. They require less floor space and are suitable for insert molding. On the other hand, horizontal type machines have a horizontal mold clamping system, allowing for easier access to the mold and better automation options. They are typically used for larger production runs and are more versatile in terms of mold designs and configurations.</p></p>
