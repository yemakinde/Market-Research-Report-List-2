<p><p>Laser communication equipment market includes various types of components such as transmitters, receivers, modulators, and demodulators. Transmitters are devices that generate and send out laser signals, while receivers capture and interpret incoming signals. Modulators are used to encode data onto the laser signal, while demodulators extract the data from the received signal. These components work together to enable high-speed and secure data transmission through laser technology in various applications, including telecommunications, satellite communication, and military operations.</p></p>
