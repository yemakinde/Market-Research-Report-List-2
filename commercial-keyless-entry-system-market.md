<p><p>Commercial keyless entry systems utilize various technologies to provide secure access to buildings and facilities. The market types include RFID, NFC, Bluetooth, fingerprint, and others. RFID systems use radio frequency identification for access control. NFC systems enable communication between devices within close proximity. Bluetooth technology allows for wireless access using mobile devices. Fingerprint recognition systems provide biometric security. Other types of keyless entry systems may include facial recognition or iris scanning technology. These technologies offer diverse options for commercial access control solutions.</p></p>
