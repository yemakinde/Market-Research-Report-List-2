<p><p>Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer (OADM) market consists of two main types: Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer. Fixed OADM allows for a specific wavelength to be added or dropped at predetermined locations along the network, while Reconfigurable OADM provides flexibility to dynamically add, drop, or pass through multiple wavelengths at different locations. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing network performance and facilitating efficient wavelength management in optical communication systems.</p></p>
