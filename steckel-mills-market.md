<p><ul><li>Single Stand</li><li>Twin Stand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steckel Mills are used in the production of hot-rolled coil by combining the processes of plate rolling and strip rolling into a single machine. The market for Steckel Mills can be categorized into two types: Single Stand and Twin Stand. Single Stand Steckel Mills consist of just one stand for rolling, while Twin Stand Steckel Mills have two stands for higher production capacity. Single Stand Steckel Mills are suitable for smaller production volumes, while Twin Stand Steckel Mills are used for higher volume production.</p></p>
