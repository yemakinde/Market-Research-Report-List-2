<p><ul><li>Spar-Buoy</li><li>Semi-submersible</li><li>Tension Leg Platform (TLP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating offshore wind power market types include Spar-Buoy, Semi-submersible, and Tension Leg Platform (TLP). Spar-Buoy type involves a cylindrical floating structure connected to a seabed anchor. Semi-submersible platforms have multiple columns connected by a floating platform. TLPs use taut mooring lines to keep the structure stable. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, with factors such as water depth, wind conditions, and cost influencing the choice of platform for a particular offshore wind power project.</p></p>
