<p><p>Coaxial Detector Market Types include Type P and Type X. Type P detectors are usually used in communication systems where phase modulation is commonly employed. They offer high sensitivity and precision in detecting small signals. On the other hand, Type X detectors are used in applications where high accuracy and linearity are required, such as radar and instrumentation systems. They provide excellent performance in terms of frequency response and dynamic range, making them suitable for demanding measurement tasks.</p></p>
