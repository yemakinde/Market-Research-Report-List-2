<p><ul><li>Straight Track Tamping Machines</li><li>Points and Crossing Tamping Machines</li><li>Multi-purpose Tamping Machines (MPT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tamping machines are used in railway maintenance to ensure the proper alignment and stability of tracks. Straight track tamping machines are used for regular maintenance on straight sections of track. Points and crossing tamping machines focus on maintaining the intricate junctions where tracks intersect. Multi-purpose tamping machines (MPT) are versatile and capable of performing various maintenance tasks on different types of tracks. Each type of tamping machine serves a specific purpose in the railway maintenance industry, ensuring the safety and efficiency of train operations.</p></p>
