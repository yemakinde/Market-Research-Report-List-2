<p><p>Logic output photocouplers are commonly classified into three types based on their output characteristics: AC, DC, and Logic Inverter. AC photocouplers provide alternating current output signals, DC photocouplers provide direct current output signals, and Logic Inverter photocouplers convert the input signal logic levels. Each type of photocoupler caters to different applications and requirements in industries such as telecommunications, automotive, medical devices, and industrial control systems. The choice of the appropriate type of photocoupler depends on the specific application and desired output signal.</p></p>
