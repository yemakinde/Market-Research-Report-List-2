<p><p>Whole slide scanners are devices used in digital pathology to digitize entire glass slides for viewing and analysis on a computer screen. There are two main types of Whole Slide Scanner markets - Brightfield and Fluorescence. Brightfield scanners use traditional staining techniques to create high-resolution images of tissue samples, while Fluorescence scanners use fluorescent labeling to detect specific molecules within a sample. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in modern pathology by providing detailed and accurate imaging of biological tissues for research and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
