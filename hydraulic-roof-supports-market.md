<p><strong>The Hydraulic Roof Supports Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chock Support</li><li>Shield Support</li><li>Chock Shield Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic roof supports are categorized into three main types: Chock support, Shield support, and Chock shield support. Chock support systems consist of hydraulic jacks that press against the roof and floor of the mine to prevent collapses. Shield supports provide roof stability by forming a protective barrier using hydraulic shields. Chock shield supports combine both chock and shield support functions to ensure enhanced roof support and stability in mining operations. Each type offers unique features and benefits for ensuring the safety and efficiency of underground mining operations.</p></p>
