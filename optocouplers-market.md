<p><p>Optocouplers, also known as opto-isolators or optical couplers, are components used to transfer electrical signals between two isolated circuits by using light waves to accomplish this task. They consist of a light source, typically an LED, and a photosensitive receiver, such as a phototransistor or photodiode, within a single package. Optocouplers provide galvanic isolation between input and output signals, protecting sensitive components and preventing signal interference.</p><p>The Optocouplers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period.
