<p><ul><li>Extracellular Agents</li><li>Blood Pool Agents</li><li>Hepatobiliary Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gadolinium based contrast media market includes three main types: extracellular agents, blood pool agents, and hepatobiliary agents. Extracellular agents are the most commonly used, primarily for imaging blood vessels and organs. Blood pool agents have a longer circulating time in the blood, making them ideal for vascular imaging. Hepatobiliary agents are specifically designed to enhance visualization of the liver and bile ducts. Each type of contrast media serves a unique purpose in enhancing the quality of medical imaging studies.</p></p>
