<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors are available in two main market types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel that slides open to allow entry or exit, while double leaf doors are made up of two panels that slide open simultaneously. Single leaf doors are ideal for smaller spaces where space efficiency is key, while double leaf doors are more commonly used in larger areas or where wider openings are required. Both types of doors provide airtight sealing, noise reduction, and smooth operation for various commercial and healthcare applications.</p></p>
