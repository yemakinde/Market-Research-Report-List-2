<p><strong>The Traffic Signal Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized Adaptive Traffic Signal Controller</li><li>Fixed Time Traffic Signal Controller</li><li>Actuated Control Traffic Signal Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Traffic Signal Controller market can be classified into three main types: Centralized Adaptive Traffic Signal Controller, Fixed Time Traffic Signal Controller, and Actuated Control Traffic Signal Controller. Centralized Adaptive Controllers use real-time traffic data to adjust signal timings for optimal traffic flow. Fixed Time Controllers operate on pre-set schedules without adjusting to traffic conditions. Actuated Control Controllers use sensors to detect vehicle presence and adjust signal timings accordingly. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different scenarios based on traffic patterns and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
