<p><p>Medium Voltage Instrument Transformers are classified into two types: Low Current Transformers (LCT) and Low Voltage Transformers (LVT). LCTs are used for measuring low current levels typically up to 5000A, while LVTs are designed for measuring relatively low voltage levels, usually up to 35kV. Both types play a critical role in the power industry by accurately transforming high voltage and high current levels into manageable levels for measurement and protection purposes. These instrument transformers ensure the safety and efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
