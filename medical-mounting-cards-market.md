<p><p>Medical mounting cards are used for mounting medical devices such as surgical instruments, catheters, and tubing for sterilization and storage. The two main types of materials used in medical mounting cards are High-density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Solid Bleached Sulfate Coated Paperboard. HDPE cards are durable, moisture-resistant, and suitable for autoclaving. On the other hand, Solid Bleached Sulfate Coated Paperboard cards have a smooth surface and are suitable for printing detailed instructions or logos. Both materials offer protection and organization for medical devices.</p></p>
