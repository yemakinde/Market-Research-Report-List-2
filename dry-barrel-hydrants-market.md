<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Mechanical Joint Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry barrel hydrants are classified into two main types based on their connection method: Flange Type and Mechanical Joint Type. Flange Type hydrants are equipped with flanges that are bolted to the underground pipeline, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. On the other hand, Mechanical Joint Type hydrants utilize a grooved mechanical joint to connect to the pipeline, ensuring a sturdy and reliable link. Both types of hydrants are commonly used in municipal water systems to provide a source of water for firefighting purposes.</p></p>
