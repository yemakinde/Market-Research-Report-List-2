<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Welding Type</li><li>Groove Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pump connectors come in three main types: flange, welding, and groove. Flange type connectors are designed with flanges at each end to bolt onto pumps or piping systems. Welding type connectors are welded directly onto pipes for a secure connection. Groove type connectors have grooves that are clamped onto pipes using bolts. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific application and system requirements. The choice of connector type is often determined by factors such as installation ease, pressure ratings, and system compatibility.</p></p>
