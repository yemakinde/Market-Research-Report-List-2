Both types are commonly used in industries such as power generation, oil & gas, and HVAC systems to regulate the flow of gases in chimneys and exhaust stacks. The market for stack dampers is driven by the increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions and strict environmental regulations on emissions.</p></p>
