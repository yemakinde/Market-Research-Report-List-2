<p><p>Gate openers come in two main types: sliding gate openers and swing gate openers. Sliding gate openers are ideal for areas with limited space as they slide horizontally to open and close. They are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings. Swing gate openers, on the other hand, are more traditional and operate by swinging open either inward or outward. They are popular in residential properties and provide a classic aesthetic. Both types offer convenience and security for property owners.</p></p>
