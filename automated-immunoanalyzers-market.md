<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully Automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated immunoanalyzers can be categorized into two main types: Semi-automated and Fully Automated. Semi-automated systems require some manual intervention by the operator, such as sample loading and result interpretation. On the other hand, Fully Automated systems perform all steps of the immunoassay process automatically, from sample handling to data analysis, with minimal human involvement. Fully Automated systems are preferred for high throughput laboratories due to their efficiency and accuracy, while Semi-automated systems are suitable for smaller facilities with lower testing volumes.</p></p>
