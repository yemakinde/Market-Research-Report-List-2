<p><p>Electric hydrostatic test pumps come in two main types: Double piston type and Three cylinder plunger type. </p><p>Double piston type pumps use two pistons to create pressure for testing purposes. These pumps are known for their high efficiency and reliability. </p><p>Three cylinder plunger type pumps, on the other hand, use three plunger cylinders to generate pressure. These pumps are often preferred for their high-pressure capabilities and precision in testing various hydraulic systems. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries requiring accurate and high-pressure testing.</p></p>
