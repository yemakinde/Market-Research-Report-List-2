<p><p>Electrochemical Instrumentation Market consists of various types of instruments such as Potentiostats/Galvanostats, Ion Chromatographs, Conductivity Meters, Salinity Meters, Titrators, pH Meters, and others. Potentiostats/Galvanostats are used to control the voltage and current in electrochemical cells. Ion Chromatographs analyze ions in a sample. Conductivity Meters measure the ability of a solution to conduct electricity. Salinity Meters measure the salt content in a solution. Titrators are used for chemical analysis. pH Meters measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. Other instruments include electrochemical sensors and analyzers.</p></p>
