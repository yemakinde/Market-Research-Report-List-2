<p><p>Angiography Injectors are devices used during medical imaging procedures to inject contrast agents into a patient's blood vessels. There are two main types of Angiography Injectors- Single-head and Dual-head. Single-head Angiography Injectors have one injector head, while Dual-head Angiography Injectors have two injector heads, allowing for simultaneous injection into multiple vessels or rapid sequential injections. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different situations depending on the specific imaging requirements of the procedure.</p></p>
