The 16-bit ECU offers basic control functions, while the 32-bit ECU provides improved precision and performance. The 64-bit ECU is the most advanced option, offering the highest level of processing power and capabilities. Each type caters to different needs in the market, with the 64-bit ECU typically being used in high-performance applications where precision and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
