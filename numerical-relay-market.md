<p><p>Numerical relays are used for various types of protection in electrical systems. Overcurrent protection relays are designed to trip when the current exceeds a set limit. Arc protection relays detect and extinguish arcs to prevent damage. Motor protection relays monitor motor parameters to prevent overheating and overload. Transformer protection relays protect transformers from faults. Capacitor bank protection relays monitor and control capacitor banks. Generator protection relays protect generators from anomalies. Other types of market include busbar protection, line protection, and substation automation.</p></p>
