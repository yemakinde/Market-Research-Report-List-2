<p><p>Solar water pumps market can be categorized into four main types based on their application and design: DC Surface Suction pumps, AC submersible pumps, DC submersible pumps, and AC surface pumps. DC Surface Suction pumps are ideal for situations where water needs to be lifted from a shallow water source. AC submersible pumps are used for deep water sources, while DC submersible pumps are designed for off-grid applications. AC surface pumps are suitable for pumping water from a surface source to a higher elevation.</p></p>
