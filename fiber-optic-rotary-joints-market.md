Single-Channel joints are designed to transmit signals across a single channel, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution for applications that require limited signal transmission. Multi-Channel joints, on the other hand, are capable of transmitting multiple channels of data simultaneously, making them ideal for more complex systems that require the transmission of multiple signals.</p></p>
