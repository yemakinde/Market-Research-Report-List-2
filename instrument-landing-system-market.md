<p><p>The Instrument Landing System (ILS) is a ground-based instrument approach system that provides guidance to aircraft for landing during adverse weather conditions, low visibility, or at night. ILS helps pilots to align their approach with the runway for a safe landing.</p><p>The Instrument Landing System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.60% during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>Instrument Landing System CAT I</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT II</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT III</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instrument Landing System (ILS) market consists of three main types: CAT I, CAT II, and CAT III. CAT I is the basic system that provides guidance to pilots for a decision height of 200 feet above the runway. CAT II offers lower decision heights down to 100 feet, while CAT III provides guidance for landing in visibility as low as 50 feet. The differentiation in these categories lies in their ability to handle varying levels of visibility and weather conditions, catering to different airport and aircraft requirements.</p></p>
