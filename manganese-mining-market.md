<p><p>Manganese mining primarily focuses on four market types: Braunite, Pyrolusite, Psilomelane, and Rhodochrosite. Braunite is a rare mineral containing high levels of manganese, primarily found in South Africa. Pyrolusite is the most abundant manganese mineral, commonly mined in countries like Ukraine and Brazil. Psilomelane is known for its botryoidal crystal structure and is found in regions like Arizona and Australia. Rhodochrosite is valued for its distinctive pink color and is mined in places like South Africa and Peru.</p></p>
