Silver alginate dressings contain silver ions which have antimicrobial properties, making them effective in preventing infection and promoting healing. Calcium alginate dressings are made from seaweed-derived alginate and are highly absorbent, creating a moist environment that is conducive to wound healing. Both types of natural alginate dressings are widely used in healthcare settings.</p></p>
