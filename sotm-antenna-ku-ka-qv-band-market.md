<p><p>SOTM antennas are used in various market types such as Ku Band, Ka Band, and Q/V-Band. Ku Band antennas operate in the frequency range of 11.7-12.7 GHz and are commonly used in satellite communications. Ka Band antennas operate in the frequency range of 26.5-40 GHz and are known for their high data transmission rates. Q/V-Band antennas operate in even higher frequency ranges of 37-42 GHz and are used for high-capacity communications. These antennas cater to different market needs and requirements in the satellite communication industry.</p></p>
