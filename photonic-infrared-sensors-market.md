<p><p>Photonic Infrared Sensors Market can be categorized into three main types based on the wavelength range they detect: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors operate in the wavelength range of 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, MWIR sensors detect wavelengths of 3 to 5 micrometers, while LWIR sensors detect wavelengths of 8 to 15 micrometers. Each type of sensor has its own unique applications and capabilities in industries such as security, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
