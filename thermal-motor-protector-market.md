<p><ul><li>Low Sensitivity</li><li>High Sensitivity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal motor protectors can be classified into two main types based on their sensitivity level: low sensitivity and high sensitivity protectors. Low sensitivity protectors are designed to provide protection against slight temperature fluctuations and are ideal for applications where minor overheating is expected. On the other hand, high sensitivity protectors are more responsive to temperature changes and provide more advanced protection against significant overheating. Both types of protectors play a crucial role in preventing motor damage and ensuring safe operation in various industrial settings.</p></p>
