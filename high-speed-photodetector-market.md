<p><p>High Speed Photodetector Market consists of two main types, Photon Detector and Thermal Detector. Photon detectors are based on detecting incoming photons of light, converting them into electrical signals. They are commonly used in applications that require high sensitivity and fast response times. On the other hand, Thermal detectors work by measuring the temperature increase caused by the absorption of light. They are known for their high-speed performance and are often used in industrial and scientific applications where accurate detection is crucial.</p></p>
