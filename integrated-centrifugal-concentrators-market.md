<p><p>Integrated centrifugal concentrators are available in two main types: concentrator with rotor and concentrator without rotor. </p><p>Concentrators with rotor utilize centrifugal force to separate materials of different densities, while concentrators without rotor use gravitational force for the separation process. Both types are designed to efficiently extract valuable minerals or substances from slurry or liquid mixtures. These concentrators are widely used in industries such as mining, food processing, and pharmaceuticals for their ability to enhance concentration levels and increase productivity.</p></p>
